Challenge

The founder of Luxury Lash & Browtique was preparing to start running lash technician training. The client needed 6 illustrated diagrams that are clear and descriptive as well as fits the brand identity.

Background

Luxury Lash & Browtique is a women’s beauty boutique in Orleans that started in early 2019. They offer a variety of services including eyelash extension training. “We are proud to provide Accredited training to become a Lash Technician or upgrade your current skills, as well as provide Accreditation towards becoming a Vanity Pro (Microcurrent technology aka the workout for your face!).” — Luxury Lash & Browtique

Process

Final Products

Each diagram incorporates the brand colours and font, making everything look like a cohesive set. The application of those elements as well as the arrangements make it easy to identify the highlights of every composition. The client was satisfied with these results and now uses these custom illustrations to help teach her class.
